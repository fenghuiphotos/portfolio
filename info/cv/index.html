Artist Statment

Photography used to feel like just a tool for documentation to me—a way to capture a single moment in a story. But now, it has become a language of expression. I try to convey my emotions and thoughts through the vocabulary of images. These visuals are no longer (.) - they’ve turned into (!) and (?).
