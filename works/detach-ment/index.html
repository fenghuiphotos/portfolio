Detach Ment

I have never felt so deeply that I am far apart from the soul, but my existence is so real.

-- Albert Camus

These photos are taken from the city where I live and its surrounding areas, including people living in the city and some simple landscapes. I have been taking photos since 2024. At first, I wandered aimlessly on the street and shot photos. Later, I thought that perhaps I could shoot under a certain theme. I thought about what theme to use for a while, and eventually decided to use my current emotional state as the theme.

I have always hoped to live in a way I like, but the reality is far from my dream. I constantly oscillate between dreams and reality, and the entanglement between them has made me increasingly distant from those around me. Gradually, I have created a self-contained world for myself, which cannot integrate with the recognized larger world. I live in this world while watching the larger world, wanting to integrate but also maintaining a certain distance.

I hope these images can reflect my own feelings, and perhaps there are people who have shared my experiences and can resonate with me after viewing these photos.
